## Don't learn, just use!

Loqu is an AI-powered social platform for language learners.

We flip traditional language learning from "learn-then-use" to "use-then-learn" - helping users start conversations from day one with AI assistance.

## What We Do

- Social platform with AI-integrated language support
- Dual-language Twitter-like experience  
- Content generation for different skill levels
- Gamified experience for sustained engagement

## Product

Available on [App Store and Play Store](https://loqu.xyz)
Currently supporting English/Korean and Korean/Japanese language pairs
